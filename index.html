RICK RIORDAN

Rick Riordan is a renowned American author whose imaginative storytelling has captivated readers worldwide. Best known for his Percy Jackson & the Olympians series, Riordan has brought ancient mythology into the modern world, creating unforgettable characters and thrilling adventures. His works have not only sparked a love for mythology in young readers but have also become a global phenomenon, translated into numerous languages and adapted into movies and television series. With a unique blend of humor, heart, and heroism, Rick Riordan continues to inspire new generations of readers.
